We Are AGCB. A Generic Christian Band. It may sound lame, but we aren’t here for the fame. We are here simply to bring worship music to everyone. Whether its in English, Spanish, Zulu, we want to sing it all so we can worship with all the people of the world. As brothers and sisters in Christ we know that someday all our voices will become as one as we sing praises to our Father in Heaven. We are all from different cultural backgrounds, denominations, and family dynamics, but at the end of the day: we are all family. So come on down to where we are, and join us and our growing family in worship!
Love,
Abbi, Adam, Grace, Gabe, Cristina, & Cora
